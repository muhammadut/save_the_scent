# Spec: Atmosphere Architect Landing Page

## Overview
A high-end, immersive landing page for SaveTheScent. The goal is to provide an "Awwwards-level" experience that transforms a visitor's perception of scent from a utility to an essential "invisible layer" of event design.

## Functional Requirements
- **Hero Section:** Full-screen optimized video background with "Surreal Transformation" visuals.
- **Scrollytelling:** 
    - **The Science:** Text reveals and parallax imagery explaining scent-memory.
    - **The Service:** Visual step-by-step of the "Atmosphere Architect" process (Arrive, Awaken, Disappear).
- **Scent Grid:** Interactive card layout for signature fragrances (e.g., New York Mystify).
- **Immersive Details:** Shared-element transition from grid card to full-screen scent immersion.
- **Inquiry Flow:** Sleek contact form integrated with EmailJS.

## Technical Requirements
- **Next.js (App Router)** for performance and structure.
- **GSAP + ScrollTrigger** for complex scrollytelling timelines.
- **Framer Motion** for UI/Card transitions.
- **Lenis** for smooth momentum scrolling.
- **Responsive Design:** Highly optimized for both Mobile and Desktop (60fps performance).

## Visual Style
- **Typography:** Editorial New (Serif) & Geist (Sans).
- **Color:** Monochrome & Moody (Black/Off-White/Charcoal).
- **Textures:** Grainy gradients and noise overlays.
